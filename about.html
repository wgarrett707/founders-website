About

Founders is Illinois' premier student entrepreneurship organization. We bring together ambitious students from every major, every year, and every background to build, learn, and grow together. Through regular events, workshops, networking sessions, and mentorship opportunities, we provide the resources and community support needed to help student entrepreneurs turn their ideas into reality. Whether you're just starting to explore entrepreneurship or you're already building your next venture, Founders is here to support your journey at every step of the entrepreneurial process.
